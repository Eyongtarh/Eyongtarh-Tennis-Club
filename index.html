Types Of Courts Available

Grass Court

Our grass court is in a warm climate region. Generally, it is advicable for grass tennis courts with such climatic conditions to use bermudagrass. This is the main reason our grass court is bermudagrass as it is drought tolerant. Out door only.

Clay Court

Our clay court is made of TOP CLAY® technology.It is designed to withstand intensive outdoor use, all year round. The unique technology of TOP CLAY® creates an uniform, homogeneous and attractive hybrid clay tennis court. This helps to prevents holes and cracks in the surface. Out door only.

Hard Court

Our hard court is made of asphalt material and covered with acrylic resins to seal the surface and for marking the playing lines. This also provides some cushion on the surface. We have one indoor and one out door for the hard court.
