Areas Of Operation

Children Trainee

We have adopted a pedagogy that the International Tennis Federation has developed. That is, balls, rackets and playing fields are adapted to the level and age of the players. The Play & Stay concept means that we use different balls that bounce differently, fast and high depending on the height and level of development of the players.

The result has been that our players relatively and quickly develop a better physique and footwork, better and more functionalstrokes and better tactics where it is possible to play "real tennis duels" at a relatively early age. This in turn creates joy for the game and that is exactly what is our most important goal. This is our approach. Book time with us to get you started.

Recreational Player

We provide adequate guidance to students as well as energetic pensioners who can be accommodated today within the framework of our program for recreational and exercise purposes. Our goal is to always offer tennis training in many different forms and something for non competitive players.

We offer courses for beginners to more advanced players. This is in the form of weekly courses, private lessons in groups or individually and exercise series. Book time so you can begin your journey.

Competitive Player

Our competition activities includes our representative teams, the competitive juniors as well as our competitive seniors and veterans. Our goal is to give children and young people who have an interest in tennis and especially the competitive aspect a solid and well thought out sports education of the highest quality.

We want to offer the best development environment and create the conditions for players who want to make a tennis investment with the goal of reaching regional, national or international top levels.
